RecordDrive generated-preview disclosure revocation validation
Date: 2026-07-23
Patched application version: 2.0.4

Finding
-------
The original XLSX/ZIP/7z preview route performed one final authorization check and
then returned the complete preview through res.json(preview). A permission/session
revocation that happened after that one-shot write began could not stop the queued
JSON body. The patch uses streamProtectedBuffer() with a 16 KiB production chunk,
a fresh current-state authorization check before every chunk, a periodic fail-closed
check while stalled, and writable-stream backpressure.
